Car Stunts 3D Turbo Racing Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Car Stunts 3D Turbo Racing handles player information. It is written in plain language and applies to the iOS release distributed on the App Store.

1. Information usage

The game is designed for quick arcade sessions and generally operates without requiring personal profile data from players to start racing.

2. Gameplay data

Progress such as unlocked stages, completion state, and basic in-game preferences may be stored locally on the device so your racing status can persist between sessions.

3. Device and performance diagnostics

To maintain stability and improve level performance, technical diagnostics may be processed in aggregated form, including crash context, device model type, and app version signals.

4. Ads and reward mechanics

Some versions may include ad-supported features or optional rewarded actions. Ad content and ad measurement are handled by integrated ad service providers under their own policies and your platform privacy settings.

5. Platform services

App download, billing, and account-level controls are provided by Apple platform services. Those interactions are governed by Apple's terms and account settings.

6. Policy updates

This policy can be updated when game systems, legal requirements, or platform rules change. The newest published version replaces prior text.

  • No personal contact details are published on this page.
  • No external marketing website is referenced here.

For the latest App Store privacy disclosure, always check the store listing tied to the exact version installed on your device.
